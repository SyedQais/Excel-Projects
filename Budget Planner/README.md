


# 📊 Annual & Monthly Budget Planner (Excel / Google Sheets)

A comprehensive **Annual and Monthly Budget Planner** designed to help individuals track income, expenses, savings, bills, and debts effectively. This planner provides automated calculations and visual insights to make personal finance management simple and organized.

---

## 🧾 Overview

This project includes:

* Monthly budget planning sheets
* An automated annual summary dashboard
* Income, expense, savings, bills, and debt tracking
* Visual charts and cashflow summaries
* A sample sheet to guide first-time users

⚠️ **Important:** The planner relies heavily on built-in formulas. Editing protected or colored cells may cause incorrect results.

## 📝 How to Use the Monthly Planner

### Step 1: Open a Month

Select the month you want to work on (e.g., January).

At first, charts may appear empty — this is normal.

---

### Step 2: Enter Values (White Cells Only)

#### 🔹 Budget / Estimated

Your planned amount for income or expenses.

**Example:**
Estimated freelancing income = `250`

---

#### 🔹 Actual

The amount you actually earned or spent.

**Example:**
Actual freelancing income = `300`

---

#### 🔹 Left

Automatically calculated difference:

* 🟢 Green → Positive
* 🔴 Red → Negative
* 🟠 Orange → Zero

---

## 💰 Planner Sections Explained

### Income

* **Primary Income**
* **Secondary Income**

Totals are automatically calculated and shown in the cashflow section.

---

### Savings

Track savings goals such as:

* Emergency fund
* Retirement
* Future purchases

Savings are automatically deducted from income.

---

### Expenses

Track daily and monthly expenses:

* Estimated vs Actual
* Automatic difference calculation

---

### Bills

Includes:

* Bill name
* Due date
* Payment checkbox to mark bills as paid

---

### Debt

Track debts such as:

* Loans
* Installments
* Due dates
* Payment status via checkboxes

---

### 📝 Monthly Reflection Box

A notes section to reflect on:

* Spending habits
* Financial improvements
* Monthly observations

---

## 📊 Visual Insights
